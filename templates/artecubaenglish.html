Art-Traces offers the unique opportunity to firsthand experience contemporary Cuban art through the exhibition of works and intimate exchange of ideas with the artists and local curators in their studios. This experience is primarily aimed at those interested in learning more about Cuban society beyond the tourist perspective. It attracts collectors of Latin American art interested in understanding contemporary art trends in Cuba. Art-Traces takes care of scheduling appointments with artists and designing programs to visit art museums, art centers, and galleries (guided by curators). It can also organize city tours to enjoy different periods of Havana's architecture. If requested in advance, we can organize art tours to other provinces of the island.
