Taking Images.

Cuban Art Traces offers two photography tours: "IMAGES TAKEN" and "TAKING IMAGES." Each can be enjoyed as a half-day or full-day tour. We also have the option to combine both, offering two or three hours of "IMAGES TAKEN" followed by a session of "TAKING IMAGES" (or vice versa). Both tours will be led by a specialist curator in Cuban art and an experienced Cuban professional photographer.
