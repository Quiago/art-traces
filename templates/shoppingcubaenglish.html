The private shopping tour is an excellent opportunity to visit antique houses, charming private craft workshops, jewelry, ceramics, fashion, and unique gifts that could be the best souvenirs of your time in Cuba. Guided by a local guide who will open the doors of private places where you can meet creators face to face and attend the process of creating each piece, and, if needed, we can facilitate the purchase or shipping process to your home if necessary. We tailor this tour to your personal interests, also including the best official stores for tobacco, rum, galleries, traditional fragrances, toys, posters, and any type of item that could be a unique memento of your trip to Cuba, helping you discover hidden places full of treasures that convey the spirit of each city.
