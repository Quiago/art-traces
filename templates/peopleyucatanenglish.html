Honey in Mani

The Yucatán Peninsula has invaluable cultural, gastronomic, architectural, and natural treasures. This destination is also an endless source for unique experiences, whether to explore sacred and historically significant places or immerse yourself in its communities. Learn to weave a hammock, attend embroidery and traditional cooking workshops, visit an artist's or artisan's studio, an architectural studio, or even extract honey directly from the hive with the authentic Mayan community.
