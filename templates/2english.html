From Popular Market
to Gourmet Kitchen:
Cooking with a Chef

Food markets in Mexico are an unmissable experience: an explosion of colors, smells, and textures blends with the vendors' calls and offers. The freshest fruits, a thousand condiments, the incredible variety of peppers, moles, sauces, cheeses, and handmade sweets set the stage for ladies in impeccable embroidered huipiles carrying their "mandados" for the family pantry. Between green recado and red mole, chaya and string cheese, it's easy to fill your bags. Everything tempts you.
That's why our tour has to start here, an amazing journey learning about flavors and traditions, hand in hand with the renowned chef Gerardo Llaca, who will help you choose the best condiments. Later, return to his kitchen and, together with him, turn them into a luxury meal to enjoy accompanied by excellent wine and even better conversation.
A graduate of the Cordon Bleu Paris culinary and hospitality school, and with extensive professional experience in more than 15 countries, Llaca is passionate about experimental and healthy cooking with local products. He is also a staunch promoter and defender of traditional cuisines. Moreover, a charming host who will make this shared meal an unforgettable experience.
