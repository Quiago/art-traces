Yucatán has a historical legacy in the architectural world like few territories in the world. Majestic ceremonial centers, capital cities, and beautiful buildings with carved stone and perfect architecture have stood in its surroundings for many centuries. The Maya people built Chichen Itzá, one of the most important ceremonial temples in archaeology and one of the wonders of the modern world. Visiting the city of Mérida is a journey into a city full of history, dreams, and hidden magic in every corner. Mérida is a blend of a contemporary city and Maya and colonial history. We can also explore the ancient streets and structures masterfully transported to our time. Exploring Yucatán and its architecture is a journey through time.
